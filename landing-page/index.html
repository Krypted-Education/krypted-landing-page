What we do?

Education on the blockchain

Learn, earn, invest

KryptEd respects all effort for valuable content in educational matters. Become part of the KryptEd ecosystem. Increase your decision-making skills. Learn, earn, invest - all on the blockchain.

Up-to-date competence

The future starts now, technological knowledge helps the world move forward. We are facing the inevitable digitalization of our world. Keep track, and ensure up to date competence in technology.

Education for everyone

You get available options for everyone around the globe. Physical academies and e-learning platform assures education available for whomever, wherever, regardless of location both geographical and in life itself.

Smart decision-making

Roadmap

Vision

KryptEd strives to create an ecosystem, supporting cryptocurrency and blockchain education through e-learning and physical academies, to educate the world.

We will achieve this by being in the front line of the fourth industrial revolution, led by a team whose key characteristics are built upon innovation, problem solving, resilient work ethics, humanity, optimism, and with limitless creativity.

Social Responsibility

We will create a generation of coders that understands the nature of smart contracts and investors that are smart decision makers.

“Decentralized Education Network” will allow equal opportunities to millions of people who have access to internet and not enough resources to understand the fourth industrial revolution.

Social responsibility is our main reason for starting this project. We vision a world with many coding schools, and with an e-learning platform available to everyone with internet and at a very low cost to no cost to user. The project will take place in developing countries with aim to serve in a minimum of ten different languages within the first five years.

We believe that blockchain technology is a revolution in human history. Starting from Bitcoin, many people are interested in getting into digital money industry without prior knowledge. There are approximately ten million people who understands java-programming language and about five thousand of these people has knowledge in blockchain technology. We want to change these numbers by being one of the front line leaders supplying value to the growth of this industry.

We are looking to be a legitimate organization with a purpose of serving people who are in need of support and who are looking to develop themselves. We want to put aside twenty percent of the commissions from platform profits, mainly for the development and education of fundamentals of blockchain technology and cryptocurrency markets. KryptEd will create a generation of coders that understands the nature of smart contracts and investors that are smart decision makers.

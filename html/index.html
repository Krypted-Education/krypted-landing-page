Our Vision

KryptEd strives to create an ecosystem, supporting cryptocurrency and blockchain education through e-learning and physical academies, to educate the world.

We will achieve this by being in the front line of the fourth industrial revolution, led by a team whose key characteristics are built upon innovation, problem solving, resilient work ethics, humanity, optimism, and with limitless creativity.

Road Map

As for 2018, marketing campaign will be launched, videos created, technical aspects shaped and generated as well as the platform’s user profile being defined.

Producers and consumer will be enabled registration and setup for profile, and paid text only courses can be sold and bought.

Metamask&Web3 will be integrated, homework support running and list of achieved certificates will be held.

Social Responsibility

We are looking to be a legitimate organization with a purpose of serving people who are in need of support and who are looking to develop themselves.

We want to put aside twenty percent of the commissions from platform profits, mainly for the development and education of fundamentals of blockchain technology and cryptocurrency markets.

KryptEd will create a generation of coders that understands the nature of smart contracts and investors that are smart decision makers.
